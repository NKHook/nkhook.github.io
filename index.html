NKHook

Welcome to NKHook, a project dedicated to reverse engineering classic Ninja Kiwi games.

BTD5-Decomp

BTD5-Decomp is the leading project in Bloons TD 5 reversal, aiming to be a full decompilation to replicate the game's source code.

NKHook5

NKHook5 aims to fix issues with Bloons TD 5, and support modding by allowing modders to create new content beyond the game's default limitations.
